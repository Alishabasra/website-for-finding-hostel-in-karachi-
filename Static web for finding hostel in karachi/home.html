    We offer a great list of facilities for your own comfort so you can have a great time at our hostel.

    1 Free Outdoor Swimming Pool

    Our outdoor swimming pool with waterfall is perfect to laze by on those 35-degree days when lining up at a museum.

    2 Free Use Of Fitness Equipment

    We have cardio fitness equipment free to all guests - to help you with your pizza and pasta guilt.
